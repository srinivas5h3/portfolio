Block chain

Department of Computer Science & Engineering organized 2 day workshop on Block Chain between 26th and 27th February, in association with APITA (JKC). Mr. Aman Sanduja, Co-Founder/CEO of "Towards Blockchain" is the resource person for this program. Students from II & III B.Tech CSE, IT & ECE participated in this program. The resource person gave an introduction to Block Chain Technology & explained how Block Chain Technology is used to create Bit Coin. He discussed various case studies explaining how Block Chain Technology could be used to give solution to real time problems.

Vishnu Nitro Championship

As a part of VALIANT-2k17, Department of Mechanical Engineering, Vishnu Institute of Technology, organised Vishnu Nitro Championship-2k17 (VNC-2k17), that attracted participants from different parts of our country. Radio controlled cars or R/C cars, as they are commonly known, are powered by small internal combustion engines fueled by a special mixture of nitro-methane, methanol and oil. VNC-2k17 provided the students a learning experience and gave them a chance to understand about chassis design, steering and suspension mechanisms. The event was also a platform for the students to interact with each other, enabled them to share and exchange their ideas.

Federal Bank Chairman's Interaction with Students

Sri Syam Srinivasan, the Chairman and CEO of Federal Bank Ltd visited Sri Vishnu Educational Society, Bhimavaram on 7th November, 2016. He addressed the students of MBA and inspired the students with his speech reflecting his vast knowledge in banking and on Indian Economy. Students took initiative to share his knowledge by volleying questions on various issues ranging from the problems being faced by Indian Banks in the wake of raising NPAs, fast changing technology, raising customer expectations, the future of Indian Banking System and the impact of outcome of US Elections on Indian Economy.
